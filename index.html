About George

George is a classically trained tenor whose emotive and powerful voice has captivated audiences in both live and recorded settings. He is known for blending tradition with modern expression and has performed at prestigious venues such as Cadogan Hall. His musical journey includes performances with the Orpheus Sinfonia and the Mozart Symphony Orchestra, bringing heartfelt vocal storytelling to every stage.
